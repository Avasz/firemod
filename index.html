FireMod

The home of aftermarket Firefox OS ROMs

FireMod is a community project of/by/for the Firefox OS lovers, curious custom ROM hackers & brave bleeding-edge testers to communicate, collaborate & share their progress.

Please Contribute to show you care.

This domain & hosting has been donated by a community member; and presently, we also need the infrastructure for hosting a Forum and a Wiki.

We'll thank you* if you can provide the infra, or donate** to help us get one.
